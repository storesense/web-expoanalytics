Key features

In-depth stand and feature analytics

Track individual attendance statistics for exhibitors and sponsored features: number of visitors, average visit time, visit distribution, conversion rate from passers-by to stand visitors and visitor return rate. Share the results with a single click.

Event visit analytics

Global event overview combines individual visit statistics to identify good and poor performers and visitor behaviour patterns. It provides the information you need to grow your event.

Visitor flow analytics

Analyse global visitor movement on a single picture. The aggregate flow display shows the number of visitors travelling down a specific passage and their average speed. Additionally, low traffic zones and hot-spots are highlighted.

Location quality analytics

The measure of quality of a location is the percentage of event visitors that pass it. The location quality display helps you identify popular locations and gain all the proof you need to justify premium pricing.
