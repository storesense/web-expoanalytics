Key features

In-depth stand and feature analytics

Track individual attendance statistics for exhibitors and sponsors: number of visitors, visit distribution, conversion rate from passers-by to stand visitors and visitor return rate are measured. Share the results with exhibitors and sponsors with a single click.

Global visit statistics

Combine the individual statistics into global event visit overview to identify good and poor performers, visitor behavior patterns and uncover the best way to grow your event.

Detailed visitor flows

Gain an insight into global visitor movements. Line thickness signifies the percentage of visitors travelling down a specific passage, while color signifies their speed. Identify low traffic zones and hot-spots at a glance.

Location quality measurement

Assign a quality to each location based on the percentage of visitors that passed it. Identify popular locations and gain all the proof you need to justify premium pricing.
